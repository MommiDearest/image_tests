  2. Rename it to exactly: index.md
  3. Replace the title on line 1 with your chapter title

  5. Save and push - the website will pick it up automatically!

The line with just dashes (---) separates the title from the description.
The title is optional - if you skip it and just write description text,
the chapter folder name will be used as the title instead.
